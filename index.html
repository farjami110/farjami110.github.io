Welcome to My Personal Site

I am Ali Farjami, a researcher and academic with expertise in Computer Science and Mathematics. I am a postdoctoral researcher at the Interdisciplinary Centre for Security, Reliability and Trust at the University of Luxembourg, working in the Sociotechnical Cybersecurity (IRiSC) group.

My research interests span several areas:

  • Normative systems and deontic logic
  • Machine ethics and and multi-agent systems
  • Logic of uncertainties and AI safety
  • Informal logics and neurosymbolic systems
